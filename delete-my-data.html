Delete your Flash Me data

Flash Me is anonymous — you don't create an account with an email or password, so the fastest and most complete way to delete your account and all your data is from inside the app.

Delete everything from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open Flash Me.
  2. Go to Settings (the gear / profile icon).
  3. Scroll to Account and tap “Delete account & all data.”
  4. Confirm. This permanently erases your account and every deck, card, and document — on your device and on our servers. It can't be undone.

Note: if you have an active Unlimited subscription, cancel it separately in your App Store or Google Play subscription settings — deleting your data here does not cancel a subscription or issue a refund.

Prefer to request it by email?

Email info@frostcard.io from the device's perspective and we'll process the deletion. (Because the app is anonymous, the in-app button is the surest way to delete your specific data.)

What gets deleted

Your anonymous account, all decks/cards/documents stored for you, your Scan balance, and any associated records. We retain only what's legally required (e.g. transaction records held by Apple or Google for their billing).
